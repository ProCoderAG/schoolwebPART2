School Life

Library

Opened in the late 1900s

Our very own library is full of the best selling books you can find. It gives a nice quiet reading space at the back and give the opportunity to print something out if need. You mighas well sit down on a table and get some work done before form time. We open at 8AM and close at 4:30PM. On a Thursday lunchtime, our libraian Hilary Payne runs book club which is aimed at the younger year (Year 7-9).

Gym

Opened in 2011

This one and only gym will make sure you keep in shape. Sadly, it is only open for Year 9-13s. Sometimes, younger years will use them in their PE lessons. We have weights, bike machines and much more. We have another room which has our very own rowing machine which is used by the rowing team to train.

Sports Hall

Opened in 1987

We use this during PE lessons and clubs. As can be seen, one of the sports we do is badminton.
